Our Roots

From a Small Seed to a Thriving Firm

It started in 1998 with a single truck and a shared vision between childhood friends, Elena and Marcus. They didn't just want to mow lawns; they wanted to understand the soul of the soil.

Both graduates of horticultural science, they combined technical precision with an artist's eye. Today, Stackly Nature is a team of forty specialists, but that original spirit of hands-on care remains in every stone we lay and every sapling we plant.

We approach every project with the same professional rigor and organic warmth that defined our very first garden in the suburbs of Portland.
